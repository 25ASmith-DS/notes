# Gerundive Verb
## Construction
2nd PP - *re* + *nd* + 2-1-2

# Gerundives of Obligation
## Construction
### Latin
1) Dative person
2) Gerundive verb
3) *sum/es/est*
### English
"\<person\> must \<verb\>"

# Gerundives of Purpose
## Construction
### Latin
1) *ad*
2) Gerundive verb
### English
The exact same as the [[Infinitives|infinitive]]
